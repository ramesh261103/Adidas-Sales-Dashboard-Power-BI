# 📊 Adidas-Sales-Dashboard-Power-BI
Interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing Adidas sales, profit, and regional performance insights.

## 🔍 Overview

This project presents an interactive **Adidas Sales Dashboard** built using Power BI to analyze business performance across regions, products, and retailers.

The dashboard provides a clear understanding of:

* Sales and profit distribution
* Regional performance (states & cities)
* Product category contribution
* Sales trends over time

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze overall sales and profit performance
* Identify top-performing states and cities
* Understand product category contribution
* Track sales trends across quarters
* Compare different sales channels

---

## 📁 Dataset

The dataset contains:

* Revenue, Profit, and Units Sold
* Product categories (Apparel, Footwear, etc.)
* State and city-level data
* Retailer information
* Time-based data (Month, Quarter)

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 🔹 Key KPIs

* Total Sales
* Total Profit
* Average Price per Unit
* Total Units Sold

### 🔹 Visualizations

* Top 5 States by Sales
* Top 5 Cities by Profit
* Sales by Product Category
* Quarterly Sales Trend
* Profit by Retailer
* Sales by Sales Method

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

* Power BI
* Microsoft Excel
* DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)

---

## 🎨 Design Approach

* Clean and minimal UI
* Consistent color theme (Blue & Orange)
* Insight-driven visualizations
* Interactive filters for better analysis

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* New York generates the highest sales among all states
* Sales peak during Q3 and slightly decline in Q4
* Online sales contribute the highest share
* A few retailers dominate overall profit

---

## 🚀 How to Use

1. Download the `.pbix` file
2. Open it using Power BI Desktop
3. Use filters to explore insights
